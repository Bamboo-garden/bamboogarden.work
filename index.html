About Garden Survivors

Garden Survivors is an exciting survival game where you'll face waves of monsters while trying to cultivate your garden. As a gardener with special abilities, you must defend your plants and grow your garden to thrive in this hostile environment.

The game combines elements of survival, strategy, and resource management, offering a unique gaming experience that will keep you engaged for hours.

Game Features

  • Exciting monster battles with various enemy types
  • Plant and grow different types of bamboo and other plants
  • Unlock special abilities and power-ups
  • Beautiful garden environments with day/night cycles
  • Progressive difficulty for continuous challenge
  • Strategic resource management

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Space bar to use your special ability
  • E key to interact with plants and objects
  • 1-4 number keys to select different tools
  • ESC to pause the game

Tips for Beginners

  • Focus on planting bamboo early to generate resources
  • Upgrade your movement speed to avoid monsters more effectively
  • Create defensive structures around your garden
  • Collect power-ups whenever possible
  • Plan your garden layout strategically for optimal growth

Game Progression

As you progress through the game, you'll face increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Each wave brings new challenges and opportunities:

Early Game (Waves 1-5)

Focus on establishing your garden and basic defenses. Collect resources and learn the game mechanics.

Mid Game (Waves 6-15)

Expand your garden and upgrade your abilities. Begin to specialize your strategy based on your playstyle.

Late Game (Waves 16+)

Master advanced techniques and face the most challenging enemies. Your garden should be fully developed with specialized areas.
